Mold

If you see or smell mold in your living area , attic , basement or crawlspace at your house or business your indoor air quality and your health may be affected . Precison Restoration Services is your locally owned and operated business that performs numerous mold remediation projects every year.

Basements, bathrooms, utility rooms, laundry rooms and kitchens are some of the areas where mold is often found. Mold can grow around and under plumbing fixtures, on cabinets , trim and doors , insulation, drywall , inside ductwork, or on joists in your attic , basement and crawlspace.

Moisture from an undetected leak can cause mold to begin growing on hidden surfaces such as the backside of drywall, paneling, in the ceiling or under flooring. Mold can begin to grow within 24-48 hours following a leak or moisture intrusion. You may suspect hidden mold if a building smells moldy, but you cannot see the source.



Water Damage

Precision Restoration's trained technicians respond quickly and professionally to your emergency to protect your structure and belongings. Our prompt emergency response to extract water and begin drying helps to prevent further damage . We use the state of the art drying equipment to complete the job from start to finish. Our customers appreciate our skilled and courteous crews arriving to begin the process of mitigation and restoration.

A project manager will guide you through the process from start to finish. He will conduct a thorough inspection of the affected area with moisture sensing equipment such as meters and infrared cameras in order to determine the directs source of the damage and the extent of the area affected. Monitoring will be performed in order to assure complete structural drying . Sanitization will be completed to prevent mold and then restoration and repairs will be done . We will use extractors, air movers, air scrubbers, dehumidifiers, wood floor drying systems and wall drying systems.

We will collaborate with your insurance company and provide you with guidance through the process so that everything is taken care of. We will provide an complete estimate for your insurance company and review your insurance company estimate to include all necessary repairs.
